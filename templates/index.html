What is Carelix

Healthcare That Knows You

Carelix eliminates the frustration of starting from scratch every time you visit a new hospital. Your health story travels with you — secure, accurate, and instantly accessible.

01

Digital Medical Card

Receive a smart, verifiable digital health card that holds your full medical profile — accessible across any Carelix partner hospital in Nigeria.

02

Unique Emergency Code

Your personal emergency code lets first responders and doctors access your critical health data instantly — blood type, allergies, chronic conditions — when every second counts.

03

Connected Hospitals

Hospitals subscribe to Carelix to manage their doctors, set card pricing, update patient records, and receive seamless payments — including via OPay and major gateways.

04

Private & Secure

Your medical data is encrypted and only accessible to authorized healthcare providers you visit. No data is shared without your knowledge or consent.

Our Mission

The Goals Driving Carelix

We are building towards a future where no Nigerian patient is treated as a stranger in a hospital.

1

Universal Health Registration

Enable every Nigerian citizen to register once at any hospital and receive a permanent health identity — no repeat forms, no lost files, no confusion between facilities.

2

Seamless Record Portability

Ensure medical records travel with the patient, not with the hospital. Your history — diagnoses, prescriptions, lab results — is visible to every doctor you authorize.

3

Emergency-Ready Healthcare

Deploy emergency codes nationwide so that in crisis moments — accidents, cardiac events, unconscious patients — first responders can act fast with full knowledge of your medical history.

4

Hospital Ecosystem Integration

Build a robust subscription model for hospitals — managing doctors, pricing, patient flows, and receiving payments — making Carelix an operating system for Nigerian healthcare.

5

Data-Driven Public Health

Aggregate anonymized health data to help the Nigerian government and health agencies make evidence-based decisions, track disease patterns, and allocate resources effectively.
